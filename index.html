📢 Competition Rules

✅ Only one player or team needs to submit each match result.
⚡ Ratings and leaderboards update automatically after every valid submission.
⏳ A minimum interval of 14 days is required before playing the same opponent in Singles or the same doubles team again.
🚫 Duplicate matches submitted within the 14-day interval will be automatically rejected and will not affect the ratings.
🤝 Doubles uses fixed teams. Changing partners resets the team rating to 1500.
📅 The 14-day interval is calculated from the submission time of the previous valid match result.
🏓 MYTT reserves the right to reject invalid, duplicate, or fraudulent match submissions to maintain a fair rating system.
💡 If you are unsure whether a match is eligible, please contact the MYTT organizer before submitting the result.
